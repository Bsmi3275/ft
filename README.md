### Installation: 
#### <p>Enter the necessary information for your workouts. It is pretty straightforward. Click "Add Workout" when you have entered everything and a message saying "successfully added" will come at the top right of your screen. Then, click "Complete," wait a few minutes, and it should produce the workout information. Click on the top left Dashboard to see your workout stats after entering a couple workouts.</p>
